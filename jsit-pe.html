Driving the Future of Precision Engineering at PE Symposium 2026!

The Precision Engineering (PE) Symposium 2026, jointly organised by the Jobs-Skills Integrator for Precision Engineering (JSIT PE) and the Singapore Precision Engineering and Technology Association (SPETA), brought together over 240 industry leaders, innovators, and aspiring talents for an energising afternoon of insights and inspiration at Nanyang Polytechnic.

Kicking off the event, Mr. Brian Tan, Regional President (SEA), Applied Materials, delivered a sharp and timely keynote on the evolving global semiconductor landscape and how AI is accelerating innovation and manufacturing complexity.

Attendees also gained fresh perspectives from our Company Spotlights featuring:
Terrence Wong, ST Engineering Ltd
Mr. Cabal Lo, ATC Globe Holdings Pte Ltd
Mr. Murali Das, Advanced Manufacturing Training Academy (AMTA)

A dynamic panel discussion featuring Mr. Bryan Toy, Executive Director, AME International, Mr. Jiang Fu Wei, Director, Panasonic Appliances R&D Centre Singapore, Mr. Muhammad Nur’Iman, SPETA Gold Awardee and Mr. Josiah Leong, Founder, The Talent People, and moderated by Ms. Jesslyn Ogmund, NYP, sparked meaningful conversations on how technology is reshaping industry roles, empowering the workforce, and strengthening talent pipelines for millennials and Gen Z.The symposium concluded with a celebratory awards segment recognising companies and young achievers who are leading the way in digital transformation, workforce development, and industry excellence.The strong momentum in Singapore’s precision engineering sector continues—and JSIT-PE is excited to help shape the next generation of talent and innovation!
